Entry Requirements

Entry Requirements for Apprenticeships

At Hot Beans Web, we believe in investing in the next generation of web developers. That's why we offer apprenticeships to individuals who are looking to start their career in web development. To be considered for our apprenticeship program, you must meet the following requirements:
The minimum Grades you need:

  • A minimum of a high school diploma or equivalent
  • Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript
  • A strong work ethic and eagerness to learn
  • Good communication skills and ability to work well in a team
  • A portfolio or examples of previous web development work (preferred, but not required)

If you meet these requirements and are interested in starting your career in web development, we encourage you to apply for our apprenticeship program. Our team of experienced developers will provide you with the training and support you need to succeed in this exciting field.

Entry Requirements for Graduate Students

Hot Beans Web is always looking for talented and motivated individuals to join our team. We welcome applications from graduate students who are interested in pursuing a career in web development. To be considered for a position at Hot Beans Web as a graduate student, you must meet the following requirements:

  • A bachelor's or master's degree in a related field (e.g. computer science, web development, graphic design)
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and other web development technologies
  • A strong portfolio demonstrating your skills and experience in web development Good communication skills and ability to work well in a team
  • A passion for staying up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in web development

If you meet these requirements and are interested in joining our team, we encourage you to apply for a position at Hot Beans Web. We offer a supportive and collaborative work environment, competitive salaries, and opportunities for growth and advancement within the company.
